Pigeon Task is a user-friendly interface for creating team tasklists that you can then email to your team.

1. Fill out the tasks for your team
2. Hit preview to see your formatted email
3. Enter your email and hit send to receive it for forwarding!

*Note: Your tasks are saved to your browser for 16 hours since your last edit!
